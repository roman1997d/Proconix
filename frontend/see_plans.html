Trial, discounts & policy

  • Free trial (any tier): pick the package you want; trial length is 14 or 30 days (set at signup). After the trial, a Proconix administrator contacts you to agree next steps — paid subscription, plan change, or end of trial.
  • Annual discount: pay 12 months, get 1 month free.
  • Scalability: move between tiers as your company grows.
  • Enterprise extras: optional integration consulting and team training.
  • Pricing is per company, not per module or per named user — the product is built as one system.
  • Tiers reflect active user count and the level of added services (support, digests, backup).
  • Every plan includes full access to all Proconix modules.
