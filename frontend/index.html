How Proconix fits into your day.

A simple flow your team can follow from the first project to every single work log.

  1. Set up your company and projects

    Add your company, managers and active projects in a few minutes. No complex configuration.

  2. Invite operatives and supervisors

    Give field teams a clear dashboard for hours, tasks, issues, uploads and work logs – on desktop or tablet.

  3. Approve, forecast and invoice with confidence

    Managers approve or reject work logs, monitor materials and QA, and always know what can be invoiced.
